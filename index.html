The CNN Fear & Greed Index scores market mood from 0 (extreme fear) to 100 (extreme greed), blending seven signals such as volatility and momentum. The contrarian idea: buy into fear, fade greed. This figure is pulled live from CNN on each refresh.
The dashboard's current call. Buy signals fire only on a real dip confirmed by fear; the greed signal means stop buying and hold cash, not sell what you own.
